Siegmund Soicke

Article

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Siegmund Soicke competed for West Germany in the men's standing Volleyball event at the 1988 Summer Paralympics, where he won a gold medal.

He also competed for Germany in men's sitting volleyball events at the 1992 Summer Paralympics (winning a broze medal), the 1996 Summer paralympics, the 2000 Summer Paralympics, and the 2004 Summer Paralympics.
